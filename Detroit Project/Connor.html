"I have no side, I was designed to stop deviants and that's what I intend to do." - Connor

Who is Connor:

Connor is an RK800 android and one of the three protagonists in Detroit: Become Human. Built as an advanced prototype, he is designed to assist human law enforcement; specifically in investigating cases involving deviant androids, becoming a successful deviants hunter. Sent to the Detroit City Police Department, Connor has been assigned to work with Lt. Hank Anderson. Throughout the course of their investigation, Connor may make discoveries about cases, himself, and become a deciding agent in tipping the coming events.

Behind the Scenes

Connor is portrayed by Bryan Dechart, who provided his likeness, the voice acting and performance capture. Jean-Charles Rousseau did motion capture stunts for Connor. Connor's serial number was #687 899 150. Connor's serial number in the finished game, #313 248 317-51, was decided on by his actor Bryan Dechart: 313 for the Detroit area code, 248 for his home area code (Novi, Michigan), 317 for his birthday, 5 for the number of people in his family, and 1 as it is the first Connor. Connor doing his quarter coin tricks was motion-capture acted by Bryan Dechart, although without the coin, which was added in later.
